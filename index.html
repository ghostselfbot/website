Features

Ghost is a powerful selfbot that offers a wide range of features to enhance your Discord experience.

Customisation

Ghost offers highly customisable image embeds. Your command will reply with a fully customised image embed. Custom title, colour, photo, footer, etc...

Open Source

Ghost is completely open source and free to use! Feel free to contribute or fork the project on GitHub.

150+ Commands

There are over 150 commands available in Ghost, covering everything from moderation to fun and utility commands.

Advanced Tools

Ghost includes advanced tools to better control your Discord account and manage your server. Backups, logs, global message search, etc...

Fast Performance

Ghost is built to ensure that your commands are executed quickly and seamlessly, providing a smooth user experience.

Ongoing Development

Ghost is constantly being improved with new features and enhancements, ensuring you always have the best experience.

Surveillance

Global message lookup tool that allows you to search through every message a user has ever sent in servers you share.
